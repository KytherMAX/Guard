Privacy Policy

Download Guard does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

The extension operates locally in the user's browser and only monitors file downloads to identify and block potentially dangerous files such as executables, scripts, and archives.

No information about browsing history, personal identity, or user activity is collected or transmitted.

No data is sold, transferred, or used for any purpose outside the core functionality of the extension.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer.
